About

TamghArt is a Moroccan DJ and cultural voice rooted in the surf and salt of Taghazout. Her sets travel through melodic techno, chill house, and open-air soul, music that breathes with the crowd, the coastline, and the hour. Every mix is a conversation with the moment, never a formula.

Beyond the decks, she is co-founder of SoundSister Morocco, a collective championing women in the country's evolving electronic music scene, building community, visibility, and space where it's long been needed.
